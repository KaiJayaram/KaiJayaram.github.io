Work Experience

Amazon Web Services Snowball
SDE Intern
June 2018 - September 2018

For the summer of 2018 I worked on Amazon's Snowball team which helps customers move data on a petabyte scale much faster than what would be possible over a normal internet connection. (More on Snowball can be found on their webpage)

As an intern I was given ownership over my own project to be delivered in the timespan of my 12 week internship. I designed, implemented, tested and deployed the project under the guidance of my mentor and manager. My project was a revamp of the existing backend for ordering a device from the snowball service. It involved automating and updating an existing fraud prevention process (designed to ensure customers do not steal the devices), automating kicking off certain customer-specific tasks for each device, and implementing proper logic for error handling in the event of a customer failing to meet fraud check criteria, or an unexpected service failure.

Once deployed my project delivered the following improvements to Snowball service:

  • Greatly reduced the operational burden of ordering a Snowball on the Snowball team.
  • Left in place a robust system designed to handle failures at any step of the process
  • Decreased risk to Snowball team by updating older anti-fraud procedures.
  • Reduced amount of time between a customer ordering a device and the device being shipped.

GLXY
Data Analyst / Andriod Developer
